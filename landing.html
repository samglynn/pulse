How we handle your data

Your numbers stay yours.

Multi-tenant by construction. Credentials in Vault, not the browser. Every row gated by row-level security at the database.

Credentials in Vault

Your POS and 7shifts API keys live in Supabase Vault. Never exposed to the browser, never in source control, rotated per tenant.

Row-level security per tenant

Every query is gated at the database by your tenant ID. Locations can't see each other's data, and users can't see locations they aren't assigned to.

Read-only by default

Rai pulls from your POS and 7shifts read-only unless you explicitly turn on write-back for schedule updates. You approve every push before it lands.
